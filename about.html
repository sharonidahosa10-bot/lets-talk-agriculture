Integrated Systems

Sharon's ecosystem—three distinct but connected layers.

Communications (Advisory), Media (Let's Talk Agriculture Forum), and Editorial (Roots and Reach) each play a role in how Sharon shapes and sustains organisational narratives.

Communications (Advisory)

Define how you're understood.

I work with organisations to define how they want to be understood. This includes shaping their narrative, clarifying their positioning, and developing a communication approach that reflects the reality of their work. From there, I support how that communication shows up across their platforms, teams, and stakeholder engagement.

The focus is simple: making sure the organisation is clear, consistent, and understood the right way.

Media (Let's Talk Agriculture Forum)

Create space to speak and engage.

Through the Let's Talk Agriculture Forum, I create space for organisations and leaders to speak about their work and engage their audience. This includes podcast, sector dialogues, and interviews that bring their work into relevant industry conversations. It is not just about visibility, but about how organisations show up, what they say, and how they are perceived in those moments.

The focus is on meaningful engagement, not just presence.

Editorial (Roots and Reach)

Document and extend the work.

Roots and Reach is where the work is documented, expanded, and made easier to engage with over time. Through written features, insights, and editorial pieces, I capture the context behind the work and translate it into content that people can read, reference, and understand more deeply.

It ensures that the work does not stop at the moment it happens, but continues to live and carry meaning.
